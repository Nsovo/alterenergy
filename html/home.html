About Company and Goals

We envision a future where sustainable energy solutions drive progress and prosperity.


Alter Energy is an energy advisory company specializing in Economic Regulatory Advisory Services, Cost Recovery Services, Revenue Value Chain, Energy Project Finance, and Financial Modeling. We are dedicated to collaborating with our clients to achieve optimal commercial solutions in electricity, renewable energy, petroleum, and gas. With over 14 years of extensive practical experience and knowledge, our client-focused approach leverages the expertise of Alter Energy practitioners to develop the best solutions while ensuring client control of the project.
